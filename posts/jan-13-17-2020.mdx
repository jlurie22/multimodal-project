---
title: First lab-confirmed cases of the coronavirus arise outside of China, initiating safety measures worldwide.
description: "Consider that for a moment: everything we see around us is assumed to have had a cause and is contingent upon."
date: January 13 2020
---

As the first cases of coronavirus were confirmed in Thailand and Japan on January 13th and 14th, public health was brought to the forefront of our minds. On January 17th, the CDC began screening passengers on flights from Wuhan arriving at San Francisco, New York City and Los Angeles. With the threat of the virus now reaching the United States, my family and I began to witness change as coworkers of my parents working in China struggled to get home.

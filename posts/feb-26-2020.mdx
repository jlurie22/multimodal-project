The CDC's Incident Manager for the COVID-19 response holds a briefing on television where she warns of the impending spread of the virus, stating that the "disruption to everyday life may be severe."

Although it took time to realize the threat of COVID-19, people reacted strongly worldwide. In my household, we resorted to excessive cleaning and being almost too cautious at the beginning of the pandemic.

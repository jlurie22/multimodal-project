title: One of the first CDC test kits is reported untrustworthy
description: "One of the first CDC test kits arrives at a public health lab..."
date: February 8 2020
---

One of the first CDC test kits arrives at a public health lab in Manhattan, New York. The laboratory reported that the test is untrustworthy.

Amid the uncertainty of the pandemic, I, among others, was concerned for my future. Being raised in the U.S. to trust the health system, their treatments and the confidence experts say they have in them, it was frightening to learn of the unreliability of the response and information regarding the coronavirus.

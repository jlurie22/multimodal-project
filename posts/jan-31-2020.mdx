date: January 31 2020
---

U.S. Secretary of Health & Human Services, Alex Azar, head of the Coronavirus Task Force, declares COVID-19 a public health emergency and announces new travel policies. The new travel restrictions applied to those travelling from China, or who have recently been in China, and are not U.S. citizens.
Click [here](https://www.npr.org/sections/health-shots/2020/01/31/801686524/trump-declares-coronavirus-a-public-health-emergency-and-restricts-travel-from-c) to learn more.
